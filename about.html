About

My Radio Class was created as part of the This American Life audio hackathon on September 19-20, 2015. While 46 million Americans listen to at least one podcast a month, there are still many people who do not. My Radio Class is an attempt to bridge this divide and encourage people new to podcasts to begin their listening journey.
