• 1. Your First Listen

Hello Dear!

My friend Betty told me such a racy story the other day. Would you like to hear it?

Wasn't that marvelous? Don't you feel like you're friends with Betty now too?

What did I just hear?
Your first podcast! That was an entire episode of the show StoryCorps. StoryCorps travels the country to record interesting stories from people like you and me.

How can I hear more?
You can listen to more episodes of StoryCorps on their website.

I thought podcasts were longer.
Most are. But podcast come in all sizes, just like TV episodes. Many of the ones I'll show you range from 15 to 45 minutes. Stay tuned to the next email to hear examples of different types of podcasts.

Do I always have to be near a computer?
Nope! There are a bunch of apps for your smart phone that make listening to podcasts really fun and easy. More on that in the next email.
